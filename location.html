Location

Amaravathi, the new Capital city of Andhra Pradesh, is located close to both Vijayawada and Guntur.The Campus is located at Inavolu about 2 km from the new AP Government Secrerariat. The campus is about 20 km from Vijayawada railway station and about 33km from Guntur railway station. The nearest airport to the campus is the Vijayawada airport
