M. Tech

About the Programme

The Integrated M.Tech (Software Engineering) programmes is a 5 year integrated course offered in all the campuses of VIT i.e. Amaravati-AP, Bhopal-MP, Chennai-TN & Vellore-TN. The programme is offered with the following objectives:

  •  To understand and apply principles of software development and evolution.
  •  To specify, verify and validate solutions to large-size problems.
  •  To plan, develop and manage large software using state-of-the-art technology.
  •  To learn emerging trends in software engineering and project management.
  •  To expose students to quality standards, CASE tools and best practices.
  •  To analyze, design and develop high-quality software solutions.

Scope of Employment

As software developers, architects, design engineers, testing and QA professionals. As project managers, consultants, solutions developers and entrepreneurs. As high-end system design engineers. As configuration managers and system analysts. As academicians and researchers.

The programme contains following courses:

Problem Solving using C / Data Structures and Algorithms / Digital Electronics and Microprocessor / Object-Oriented Programming Using C++ / Computer Architecture and Organization / Programming in Java / ObjectOriented Analysis and Design / Theory of Computation / Database Management Systems / Operating Systems / Principles of Software Engineering / Computer Networks / Requirements Engineering and Management/ Internet & Web programming / Software Architecture and Design / System Programming/ Software Construction and Maintenance / Software Project Management / Open Source Programming/ Web Services and Service Oriented Architecture / Software Testing / Design Patterns / Principles of User Interface Design / Software Configuration Management / Software Quality and Reliability / Software Metrics / Distributed Computing / Artificial Intelligence and Intelligent Systems / Information and System Security / Software Engineering Process, Tools and Methods. There are also electives from core, science and management social sciences to round one‘s personality. Further details on the courses may be obtained from http://www.vit.ac.in/academics/schools/site/cour ses

Eligibility

Nationality

The applicants for admission should be a Resident Indian National and should have studied in regular, full time and formal education schools located in India in the preceding two years. NRI/ Foreign should apply separately

Age Limit

Applicants whose date of birth falls on or after 1st July 1997 are eligible to apply. The date of birth as recorded in the High School / SSC / X certificate will be considered authentic. Applicants should produce this certificate in original as a proof of their age at the time of counselling, failing which they will be disqualified.

Qualifying Examination

A pass in Higher Secondary Examination conducted by the State / Central Board of Secondary Education or its equivalent examination with 70% aggregate in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics (PCM). The qualification criteria is relaxed by 10 % i.e. 60% in PCM for applicants belong to SC/ST and applicants hailing from Jammu & Kashmir and the North Eastern states of Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Sikkim and Tripura. Applicants belonging to SC/ST category must fill in the proforma as given in Appendix-IV and have it attested by a competent authority (The list of authorities empowered for the same is given in Appendix-IV.) Applicants hailing from Jammu & Kashmir and the North Eastern states of Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Sikkim and Tripura. They should fill in the Nativity Certificate given in Appendix-III. Applicants from open Schooling / Correspondence mode are not eligible.
