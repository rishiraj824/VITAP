Is it possible to have Branch migration after first year ?
In VIT-AP, unlike all the VIT campuses, with FFCS, Fully Flexible credit system, you have the option of having Major and Minor degree. For Example. If the student has taken admission in Mechanical Engineering, and later during the first year he developed interest and wants to study CSE Computers Science Engineering, the student can choose to study CSE courses from 2nd year. The final degree will be awarded as . This is to certify that Mr/Ms …………………………………. Has been awarded B.Tech Degree in Mechanical as Major and CSE as Minor. This will enable the student to be applying for both Core Mechanical companies for placements and also IT companies as he or she did their minor in CSE.

NOTE : Very important condition for the above opportunity is that, The candidate should be in top 3% of his or her class during the first year.

Is the 2 + 2 ITP applicable to all branches at VIT-AP ?
Yes it is applicable to all the branches at VIT-AP. International Relations: Our experience of running VIT for the past 33 years in Vellore is being put to full use at VIT-AP at Amaravati.
